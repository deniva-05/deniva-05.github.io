KAPITEL 1
SYNCHRONIZITÄT

In this chapter, the player progresses through multiple locations of the "S-23 Sierpinski", covering several floors. The player is introduced to the basic gameplay elements, first enemies (EULR (Eule)) and the first weapon (PISTOL). As the chapter progresses, the player gets to meet stronger (STAR (Starling), STCR (Storch)), trickier (ADLR (Adler), ARAR (Ara), FKLR (Falke)), shy (KLBR (Kolibri)) and adorable (MNHR (Mynah)).
The chapter ends with the player entering the mines via elevator, where a Chapter 2: Liminality awaits with new locations and challenges.
